Growing Amaryllis

Planting Amaryllis bulbs

  • Size and type of container: 5 inch, Terracotta pot.
  • Location: In dining room table with no direct sun light.
  • Medium type: Regular potting mix.
  • Watering schedule: 1-2 times a week. When surface of the soil seems dry.
